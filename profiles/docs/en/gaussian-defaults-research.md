## Overview: Gaussian Normal Distribution

In a normal distribution:
- **68%** of the population lies within ±1 standard deviation (σ) from the mean
- **95%** lies within ±2σ
- **80%** typically lies between **30-70 points** (with mean 50, σ=15)
