## PART 1: PIRSIG NEEDS

### Philosophical Background

Robert M. Pirsig's "Metaphysics of Quality" distinguishes:
- **Static Quality:** Stability, patterns, preservation
- **Dynamic Quality:** Growth, change, evolution
- **Gumption:** Inner motivation, flow, enthusiasm

### 1.1 Gumption & Flow ✅ VALIDATED

|------|------|--------|----------------------|--------|

## PART 2: OSHO NEEDS

### Philosophical Background

Osho's Tantra/Zen philosophy emphasizes:
- **Non-Attachment:** Freedom from possessive thinking
- **Yin/Yang:** Complementary polarities
- **Zorba-Buddha:** Worldly enjoyment + spirituality
- **Meditation:** Awareness in daily life

### 2.1 Non-Attachment ✅ VALIDATED

| Need | Base | Status | Scientific Equivalent | Source |
|------|------|--------|----------------------|--------|
| non_attachment_to_partner | 40 | ✅ | Secure Attachment (Low Anxiety, Low Avoidance) | Brennan et al. (1998) |
| non_attachment_to_family | 45 | ✅ | Differentiation of Self | Skowron & Friedlander (1998) |
| non_attachment_to_money | 50 | ⚠️ | Material Values (inverse) | Richins & Dawson (1992) |

**⚠️ IMPORTANT FINDING (2025-12-08):**

> **Osho's "Non-Attachment" ≠ Avoidant Attachment!**
>
> Non-attachment in Osho's sense means **Secure Attachment** with
> **low anxiety** (no clinging) AND **low avoidance** (no distancing).
>
> It is NOT "Dismissive-Avoidant" (relationship avoidance).

**Mapping Osho → Attachment Theory (corrected):**

| Osho Concept | Attachment Style | Scientific Dimension |
|--------------|------------------|---------------------|
| Non-Attachment (high) | **Secure** | Low Anxiety + Low Avoidance |
| Attachment (high) | Anxious-Preoccupied | High Anxiety |
| Avoidance (high) | Dismissive-Avoidant | High Avoidance |
